The name of the organizational site will be Simply Weather and the domain for the site will be called simplyweather.com.

The goal of yourweather.com will be to help educate and inform the users about the weather conditions in a determinated location.Additionnally it would provide weather predictions to specified locations, Also simplyweather.com will issue severe weather warnings and educational opportunities for local population. Weather-related alerts will also be announced to the site as well as the latest weather-related information and a gallery of weather-related photos for use in the community centers.
